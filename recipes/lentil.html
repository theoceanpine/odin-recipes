Lentil Sauce

Description

Warm and delicious lentil sauce that will supply you with enough of protein.

Ingredients

  • 200g of Red Lentils
  • Onion - 1 Half
  • Garlic - 2-3 cloves
  • White Mushrooms - 4-5
  • Red/Green/Yellow Pepper - 1
  • Sweet Potato - 1
  • Handful of spinach
  • Turmeric - 1tsp
  • Garam Masala - 1Tbsp
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • Stock
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the onion till brown.
  2. Add sweet potato and garlic. Fry for a few minutes.
  3. Add pepper and mushrooms. Fry for a few more minutes
  4. Add pre-cooked lentils and seasoning/stock
  5. Add a can of coconut milk and 1 can of chopped tomatoes. Cook for 10mins.
  6. Add spinach and cook for 5 more minutes.

Extras

Served with side of rice. Enjoy.
