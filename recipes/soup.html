Cream Soup

Description

Beautiful butternut squash vegetable soup to warm you up.

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash, diced - 500g
  • Onion - 1 Half
  • Yellow Curry Powder - 2 tsp
  • Garlic - 2-3 cloves
  • Cumin - 1tsp
  • Turmeric - 1tsp
  • Stock - 1 cube
  • Fresh chopped cilantro
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the onion till brown.
  2. Add butternut squash and garlic. Fry for a few minutes.
  3. Add all of the seasoning and stock.
  4. Fry for 2-4mins.
  5. Add 500ml of water. Bring to boil and then simmer until butternut squash is soft.Add water if necessary.

Extras

Serve with fresh cilantro on top and buttered toast on a side. Enjoy!
